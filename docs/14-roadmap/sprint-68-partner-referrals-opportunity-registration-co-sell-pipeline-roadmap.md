# Sprint 68 Partner Referrals Opportunity Registration Co-Sell Pipeline Roadmap

## Purpose

This document defines Sprint 68 for Partner Referrals, Opportunity Registration, and Co-Sell Pipeline foundation.

Sprint 68 should create a reusable partner growth layer for referral leads, opportunity registrations, co-sell pipeline stages, partner eligibility/readiness checks, duplicate/conflict reviews, revenue/subscription links, customer success/implementation handoffs, partner status updates, and audit history.

## Sprint Goal

Enable approved partners and platform teams to register referrals, review opportunities, coordinate co-selling, route qualified opportunities to revenue/customer success workflows, and preserve visibility boundaries.

## Why This Sprint

The platform now has partner readiness and delivery foundations:

- partner ecosystem and delivery delegation
- partner enablement and certification programs
- subscription billing and revenue operations
- customer success and account health
- implementation projects and onboarding delivery
- support operations and knowledge base
- product communication and solution blueprints

Without a shared referral and opportunity foundation, partner-sourced growth may be tracked through spreadsheets, emails, informal notes, and disconnected support/billing records.

## Work Package 1: Referral Lead Metadata

Prepare referral lead records with:

- referral key
- partner organization
- submitted by safe label
- prospect safe summary
- country/region placeholder
- status

## Work Package 2: Opportunity Registration Metadata

Prepare opportunity registration records with:

- opportunity key
- source referral
- target organization/prospect placeholder
- product/module interest placeholder
- estimated value placeholder
- review status

## Work Package 3: Co-Sell Pipeline Stage Placeholder

Prepare pipeline stage placeholders for:

- submitted
- accepted for review
- qualified placeholder
- co-sell in progress
- converted to subscription opportunity placeholder
- closed placeholder

## Work Package 4: Partner Eligibility and Readiness Check

Prepare checks for:

- partner active status
- implementer certification status
- assignment/delegation eligibility
- country/region eligibility
- solution blueprint certification placeholder

## Work Package 5: Duplicate and Conflict Review

Prepare duplicate/conflict placeholders for:

- duplicate referral warning
- existing customer/prospect conflict
- multiple partner claim warning
- internal sales ownership conflict placeholder
- partner visibility restriction warning

## Work Package 6: Revenue and Subscription Linkage

Prepare links to:

- subscription plan interest
- billing/revenue operation record placeholder
- pricing/package placeholder
- payment readiness placeholder
- renewal/expansion opportunity placeholder

## Work Package 7: Customer Success and Implementation Handoff

Prepare handoff links to:

- customer success account setup
- implementation project placeholder
- onboarding delivery project
- solution blueprint recommendation
- support readiness notes

## Work Package 8: Partner Status Updates

Prepare status update placeholders so partners can receive safe progress updates without seeing restricted tenant or internal details.

## Work Package 9: Flutter Foundation

Prepare screens or placeholders for:

- partner referral dashboard
- referral lead list
- opportunity registration list
- opportunity detail
- co-sell pipeline board
- eligibility/readiness review
- duplicate/conflict review
- revenue/subscription linkage view
- handoff view
- referral audit history

## Out of Scope

Do not implement:

- full CRM replacement
- automatic deal approval
- automatic commission calculation or payout
- legal contract management
- unrestricted partner visibility into tenant/customer records
- bypass of partner certification, tenant approval, or entitlement rules
- AI-only opportunity qualification decisions

## Completion Standard

Sprint 68 is complete when referral leads, opportunity registrations, co-sell stages, eligibility/readiness checks, duplicate/conflict reviews, revenue/subscription links, customer success/implementation handoffs, partner status updates, and referral audit history are available through permission-protected and auditable foundations.

## Final Rule

Partner referral operations should help the platform grow through trusted partners while preserving review, privacy, revenue boundaries, and audit history.
