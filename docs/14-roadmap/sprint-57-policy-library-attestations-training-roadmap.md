# Sprint 57 Policy Library Attestations Training Roadmap

## Purpose

This document defines Sprint 57 for Policy Library, Attestations, and Training Acknowledgement foundation.

Sprint 57 should create a reusable policy communication layer for policy metadata, policy versions, publication reviews, attestation requests, acknowledgement records, training assignments, completion placeholders, audience targeting, evidence links, and audit history.

## Sprint Goal

Enable organizations to publish policies, assign them to the right audiences, request attestations, track acknowledgements, link training evidence, and review overdue or missing acknowledgements.

## Why This Sprint

The platform now has governance and evidence foundations that need policy communication support:

- risk controls and compliance evidence
- governance authority and delegated approvals
- HR/workforce management
- privacy and consent governance
- provider connections and operational controls
- data portability and tenant handover
- organization networks and branch governance
- document/media library

Without a shared policy acknowledgement foundation, organizations cannot prove that users received, read, or accepted required operational guidance.

## Work Package 1: Policy Document Metadata

Prepare policy records with:

- policy key
- organization
- owner module/domain
- document reference placeholder
- category
- status

## Work Package 2: Policy Version Placeholder

Prepare version placeholders for:

- version label
- document reference
- effective date placeholder
- review date placeholder
- replaced version placeholder

## Work Package 3: Publication and Review Placeholder

Prepare publication workflow placeholders for:

- draft
- review requested
- approved placeholder
- published placeholder
- retired placeholder

## Work Package 4: Attestation Requests

Prepare attestation request records with:

- policy version
- target audience placeholder
- due date placeholder
- status
- created by

## Work Package 5: Acknowledgement Records

Prepare acknowledgement records with:

- attestation request
- user/member/worker reference placeholder
- acknowledgement status
- acknowledged at placeholder
- source channel placeholder

## Work Package 6: Training Assignment Placeholder

Prepare training assignment placeholders for:

- policy-related training
- role-based training
- branch/network training
- onboarding training
- refresher training

## Work Package 7: Audience Targeting Placeholder

Prepare target audience placeholders for:

- users
- roles
- workforce groups
- branch/network groups
- module-specific actors

## Work Package 8: Evidence Linkage

Prepare evidence links to:

- risk/control checks
- policy publication events
- acknowledgement records
- training completion placeholders
- document/media references

## Work Package 9: Flutter Foundation

Prepare screens or placeholders for:

- policy library dashboard
- policy list
- policy version detail
- attestation request list
- acknowledgement status list
- training assignment list
- overdue acknowledgement review
- policy evidence links
- policy audit history

## Out of Scope

Do not implement:

- learning management system replacement
- legal policy authoring engine
- automatic legal compliance certification
- unrestricted policy access across organizations
- bypass of privacy or document permissions
- mandatory acknowledgement without audience policy
- arbitrary script-based training rules

## Completion Standard

Sprint 57 is complete when policy metadata, versions, publication reviews, attestation requests, acknowledgement records, training assignments, audience targeting, evidence links, and policy audit history are available through permission-protected and auditable foundations.

## Final Rule

Policy acknowledgement should prove communication and acceptance without overstating legal compliance or replacing formal training systems.
