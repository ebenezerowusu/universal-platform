## Expected Output

The coding agent should produce a focused policy acknowledgement foundation for policy metadata, versions, publication reviews, attestation requests, acknowledgement records, training assignments, completion placeholders, audience targeting, evidence links, and audit-safe policy communication operations.

## Final Rule

Policy and training acknowledgement must prove communication and acceptance of guidance without pretending to be legal advice or a full learning management system. Policy documents should link to Document/Media and evidence should link to Risk/Controls where appropriate.
