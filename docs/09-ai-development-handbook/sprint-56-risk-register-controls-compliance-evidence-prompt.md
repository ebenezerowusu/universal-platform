## Expected Output

The coding agent should produce a focused risk and controls foundation for risk registers, risk assessments, control objectives, control checks, evidence links, exceptions, remediation actions, compliance checklist mappings, review schedules, and audit-safe risk operations.

## Final Rule

Risk and compliance features must track operational readiness and evidence without pretending to provide legal certification. Controls should reference existing audit, monitoring, privacy, backup, governance, and security signals instead of duplicating those engines.
